ABOUT US

Your journey to recovery starts with us

New Horizon Behavioral is an outpatient practice dedicated to improving individuals' mental health and well-being across New Mexico. Our team of Psychiatrists, Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioners (PMHNPs), licensed therapists, licensed counselors, licensed social workers, and other professionals all work together to provide compassionate, comprehensive, and evidence-based care.

We believe that every person’s journey toward mental health is unique. That’s why we offer individualized treatment plans that focus on the whole person — mind, body, and spirit. Whether someone is struggling with depression, anxiety, trauma, or a co-occurring substance use disorder, we are committed to walking alongside them every step of the way.

Our approach combines professional expertise with genuine care, creating a safe and supportive environment where clients can explore challenges, develop coping skills, and rediscover their strengths. At New Horizon Behavioral, we view every day as an opportunity for growth and healing — and we are here to help our clients embrace the possibilities that lie ahead.
