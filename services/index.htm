FAQ

What you should know about our treatment

Do you treat substance use disorders?

Yes. We provide care for individuals with co-occurring mental health and substance use disorders, including relapse prevention strategies and counseling. If a higher level of care is needed (such as detox or residential treatment), we can help connect you with trusted partners.

What ages do you see?

We serve adolescents, adults, and older adults. Our providers are experienced in treating mental health concerns across the lifespan.

How do I know if I need therapy, medication, or both?

Your provider will discuss the best treatment options during your first appointment. Some individuals benefit most from therapy alone, while others may need a combination of therapy and medication for the best results.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office for a current list of accepted plans. If you do not have insurance, we offer self-pay rates and can discuss payment options.
