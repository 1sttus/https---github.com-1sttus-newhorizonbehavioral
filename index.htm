ABOUT US

Your journey to recovery starts with us

At New Horizon Behavioral, we believe that healing is possible and that no one should walk their journey alone. Located in Albuquerque, New Mexico, our outpatient practice provides comprehensive mental health and co-occurring disorder treatment to individuals of all ages.

Our team includes Psychiatrists, Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioners (PMHNPs), Licensed Counselors, Licensed Therapists, Licensed Social Workers, and other professionals who work together to deliver person-centered care. We focus on understanding the whole person — not just the symptoms — and helping each client build resilience, find balance, and discover a healthier way forward.

Whether you are experiencing anxiety, depression, trauma, substance use challenges, or a combination of concerns, we offer a safe, supportive space where healing and growth can begin.

    FAQ

    What you should know about our treatment

    What can I expect at my first appointment?

    Your first visit is focused on getting to know you and your needs. One of our providers will complete a comprehensive evaluation, which includes discussing your mental health history, current concerns, and goals for treatment. Together, we will create a personalized care plan that works for you.

    Who will I see during my treatment?

    New Horizon Behavioral is a multidisciplinary clinic with psychiatrists, mental health nurse practitioners (PMHNPs), licensed therapists, Licensed counsellors, licensed social workers, and other professionals. Depending on your needs, you may work with one or more members of our team for medication management, therapy, or both.

    Do you provide medication management?

    Yes. Our PMHNPs are trained to diagnose mental health conditions and prescribe psychiatric medications when appropriate. We focus on safe, evidence-based prescribing and will work with you to monitor your progress and adjust treatment as needed.

    What types of therapy do you offer?

    We provide a variety of evidence-based therapy approaches, including:

    • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
    • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) skills
    • Trauma-informed therapy
    • Motivational interviewing
    • Family therapy and psychoeducation
    Our therapists work with you to select the best approach for your goals.
