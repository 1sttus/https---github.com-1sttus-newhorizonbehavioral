Treatment Programs

What can I expect at my first appointment?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive evaluation of your history, current concerns, and goals. Together, we’ll create a personalized care plan.

Who will I see during treatment?

You may meet with psychiatrists, PMHNPs, therapists, counselors, or social workers depending on your needs.

What types of therapy do you offer?

We provide CBT, DBT skills, trauma-informed therapy, motivational interviewing, and family therapy.

How do I know if I need therapy, medication, or both?

Your provider will recommend the best treatment plan after your evaluation.

How often will I need to come in?

This varies depending on your personalized treatment plan.

Patient Support

Do you treat substance use disorders?

Yes. We offer integrated care for mental health and substance use, including relapse prevention. If a higher level of care is needed, we’ll connect you with trusted partners.

What ages do you serve?

We work with adolescents, adults, and older adults.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. We follow HIPAA guidelines and protect your privacy at all times.

What if I need urgent help?

If in crisis, call 988 or visit the nearest ER. We provide crisis support but are not a 24/7 emergency facility.

Billing & Policies

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Call us for details.

What if I don’t have insurance?

We offer self-pay rates and flexible payment options.

Are my treatment costs discussed upfront?

Yes, we provide clear information about billing and policies before treatment begins.

Is there a refund policy if I discontinue treatment early?

Treatment fees cover the services already provided. While we do not issue refunds for completed sessions, we will stop billing for any future appointments you choose to cancel. Flexible scheduling and pay-per-session options are available to help you manage costs.

Preparing for Treatment

How do I schedule an appointment?

Call our office or use our online form. We’ll confirm a time that works best for you.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring ID, insurance details (if applicable), and any completed intake forms.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes, secure HIPAA-compliant telehealth sessions are available.

Do I need to stop medications or habits before arriving?

No. Please do not stop any prescribed medications or habits on your own before your visit. During your first appointment, our providers will review your current medications and lifestyle factors, then guide you safely on any needed changes.
